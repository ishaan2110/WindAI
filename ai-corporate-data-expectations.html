The Trade-offs: Advantages & Considerations

Advantages

  • Dramatically reduces time to generate reports and summaries — what took days can take minutes.
  • Handles large, complex datasets that humans would take days or weeks to process manually.
  • Consistent and repeatable analysis — no human fatigue errors or variation between analysts.
  • Surfaces hidden patterns and correlations in your data that might otherwise go unnoticed.
  • Scales effortlessly — analyse 10 rows or 10 million rows with the same ease.
  • Frees up expert staff to focus on high-value decision-making and strategic work.
  • Available around the clock — no waiting for office hours or analyst availability.

Considerations

  • Larger or more complex datasets require significantly more processing time — set realistic expectations.
  • AI outputs must always be reviewed and validated by a human expert before use.
  • Quality of output depends heavily on quality of input data — garbage in, garbage out.
  • Sensitive or proprietary data requires strict governance and approval before use with AI tools.
  • AI can confidently produce incorrect results — always sanity-check against known benchmarks.
  • May require prompt refinement and multiple iterations to get accurate, useful answers.
  • Not a replacement for domain expertise and contextual judgment — AI augments, not replaces.

Best Practices for AI with Corporate Data

Start Small

Pilot with a representative subset of your data before scaling to the full dataset. Validate results at each stage to catch issues early.

Validate Outputs

Cross-check AI results against known benchmarks, historical data, or manual spot-checks. Never distribute unvalidated AI outputs.

Clean Your Data

Better input quality equals better AI output. Remove duplicates, fix formatting inconsistencies, and handle missing values before processing.

Iterate Prompts

Refine your questions and instructions for better results. Be specific, provide context, and don't hesitate to ask follow-up questions.

Govern Sensitive Data

Follow corporate data policies at all times. Obtain necessary approvals before using confidential, proprietary, or regulated data with AI tools.

Involve Experts

AI augments — it does not replace — domain knowledge. Always involve subject matter experts to interpret, contextualise, and validate AI findings.

AI is a powerful tool — use it thoughtfully.
